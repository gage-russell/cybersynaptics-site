We started Cybersynaptics because we kept seeing the same problem: great companies stalling out because they didn't have the right technical leadership at the right time.

A founder who can't get honest answers on their architecture. A Series A company that lost its CTO and is now making high-stakes decisions by committee. A company under SOC 2 pressure with no one who's been through it before.

These aren't niche problems — they're near-universal inflection points. And the traditional answer ("hire a full-time executive") isn't always the right one, especially when timing, budget, and the talent market work against you.

## What We Do

Cybersynaptics embeds senior technical leadership into your organization:

- **Virtual CTO** — Roadmap ownership, architecture decisions, team leadership, and investor communication. All the things a great CTO does, on the engagement model that fits your stage.

- **Virtual CISO** — Security program design, compliance readiness (SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA), and threat management. Senior security leadership without waiting 6 months to hire.

- **Software Development** — Senior engineers who integrate with your team, understand your context, and ship work you're proud of. No agency overhead, no juniors posing as seniors.

## Who We Work With

We work best with companies at inflection points: post-seed startups building their first engineering org, growth-stage companies navigating compliance for the first time, and product teams that need to ship something critical without adding permanent headcount.

If that sounds like you, we'd love to talk.
